The dss-package version 3.0-SNAPSHOT-20130919 is a work in progress.
It is meant to early get feedback by the technical adviser of the DG MARKT - foremost on the design aspects.

This version aims the implementation of :
- The new validation policy with advanced report during the validation of an eSignature.
- The customisation of the validation policy.
- The new ETSI TSL standard in the TLManager.

At the current stage, all features have been covered.
However, this new version has not been fully tested yet. Minor bugs can remain and will be corrected for the version Submitted for Review.
